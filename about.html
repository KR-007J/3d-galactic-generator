About Particle Universe

A next-generation 3D particle effects generator built for creators, designers, and developers to push the boundaries of digital motion.

What is Particle Universe?

Particle Universe is an open-source visual sandbox designed to democratize high-fidelity 3D particle simulations. Built on the power of WebGL and Three.js, it bridges the gap between complex mathematical simulations and creative expression.

Whether you are building an immersive web experience, prototyping motion graphics, or exploring the aesthetics of generative art, Particle Universe provides a streamlined interface to control millions of points in real-time without writing a single line of shader code.
